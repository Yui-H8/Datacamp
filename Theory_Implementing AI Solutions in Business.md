# Implementing AI Solutions in Business
---
**Description**

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is being adopted at a faster and faster rate due to the increase in compute efficiency, decrease in compute costs, and heightened democratization of foundational models. Businesses from all industries are seeking ways to leverage AI to optimize processes, improve customer experience, and drive business value.


In this course, you'll learn how to successfully implement an AI solution for your business. You'll identify appropriate business use cases, build a proof of concept, measure success, and evaluate how to move from POC to a fully implemented solution. You'll learn how Responsible AI, security, and upskilling are equally important components.
