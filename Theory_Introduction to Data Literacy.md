### Defining data literacy
Data is all around us; we can learn so much about the world around us through data. Being data literate in today's world is important in helping us understand how data can be combined and connected to create value.

What is not a component of data literacy?
```
The ability to communicate insights effectively
〇 The ability to read and understand mathematical functions
The ability to identify potential data sources
The ability to understand how to get from data to insights
```
*Good job! It is not necessary at all to learn mathematical functions to become data literate.*

### What is insight?
What is not a characteristic of insight?
```
Insight allows us to get closer to the ideal of wisdom
Insight is valuable, and can realistically be achieved
Insight allows us to apply knowledge and take action
〇 Wisdom can be approached, but not quite fully reached
```
*That's right! Wisdom is the ideal that is just beyond our reach, but insight is the next best thing we can reach.*
