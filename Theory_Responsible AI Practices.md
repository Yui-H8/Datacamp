# Responsible AI Practices
---
**Description**

Dive into Responsible AI with this course designed to help you implement ethical AI in your organization. You'll explore Responsible AI principles, their evolution, and practical applications through real-world case studies and interactive content. Learn to navigate risks like bias, privacy, and security, and understand global AI regulations. Gain strategies for fostering an ethical AI environment with tools for risk management and governance models. By the end, you'll be equipped to lead AI initiatives responsibly, making informed, ethical decisions. Ideal for both AI professionals and newcomers. Enrol now and shape the future responsibly.
